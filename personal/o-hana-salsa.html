O-Hana Salsa

Thu, 30 Dec 2010

Posted In: personal

Tagged: deliciousbend

I was at Fred Meyer a while back, and found O-Hana Salsa. It was on sale and I noticed it's made in Bend, so I decided to try some.

It's good stuff! The label doesn't look real great, and I'm not sure what the name means, but the salsa itself is quite tasty. At this point, I'm about ready to declare it the Official Salsa of Aaron James Young. I'm sure they'll be thrilled.
