We're back!

Tue, 17 Jun 2008

Posted In: personal

Tagged: bendfamilytravel

We're back from our trip to Bend. It was a great trip. The weather was amazing (80's and sunny the whole time) and Bend seems like a pretty cool town. We have a few pictures up, just click "pictures" above.

Here's a quick run-down of the trip (links are to Google maps of trails):

Thursday: After arriving in Bend, we ran the First Street Rapids trail. This starts just a few blocks from downtown and is a dirt/gravel trail along the Deschutes river with views of mountains. Bend has lots of these kinds of trails within a few minutes of town. Thursday's dinner was at the McMenamin's downtown.

Friday's coffee was Bend Mountain Coffee. The run was atShevlin Park, a city park along the west end of town. It's got trails through the forest and feels further out than it really is. Dinner was Pizza Mondo.

Saturday's coffee was Maui Wowi (yes, that's what it is really called). Good coffee! Evelyn ran a trail from Larkspur Park to Pilot Butte, then up the butte, then cooled down back to the park. Again, it's cool that Bend has these trails in town. Dinner was... let's see... dinner was Bella Cucina, a little Italian restaurant in the Old Mill District.

Sunday's coffee was the Strictly Organic Coffee House. We ran at Shevlin Park again and stopped for smoothies from Maui Wowi on our way back into town. Dinner was Toomie's, a Thai restaurant downtown.

Monday's coffee was Backporch Coffee. They roast their own coffee and have some interesting blends. We ran on the Deschutes River Trail south of town. This was only a 10-minute drive from town! Shevlin Park was even closer. Did I mention that we were impressed with how much trail running there is in Bend? Dinner was at a seafood chain restaurant called Anthony's. It was nice!

Tuesday's coffee was Bellatazza. We then packed our stuff and left town. On the way back, we stopped in Sisters and ran the Eagle Rock Loop. And now we're home! And it's cloudy again!
