The Circle Orange Website Process

A consistent process sets Circle Orange apart from other web development agencies:

Planning

Successful web projects are well planned! We will begin with the planning process before starting design. We will answer questions such as these:

  • Who will be using your website? How will they find your site, and why will they come back?
  • How will your website help users become customers?
  • Who will be updating your website? When and how often will this happen?
  • What content will be featured on the site? What kind of content will receive regular updates? How will your site stay current?

We will work together to come up with a web strategy for you - a roadmap of how the site will evolve over time after launch. In many cases, the success of your website hinges on a strategy being followed (rather than the design of the site) so we take this step very seriously. I will continue to work with you post-launch to make sure the strategy is being implemented.

Also after site launch, we will be able to identify areas where we can improve the web strategy. Our goals are usually to increase user engagement, and we will adapt as needed over time.

What are we going to build?

During the planning stage, we'll learn about your goals and values. This is when we will decide on the features and focus of the new website.

Design

After the planning phase, we will know what we're planning to build. Now we need to design it.

I start with wireframes and prototypes. This shows what the basic layout of the pages of your website will look like and demonstrates some behaviors.

These layouts are then styled and iterated on to produce working HTML prototypes. I do not generally create static comps of websites.

We will work together to iterate on the design, always keeping our strategy in mind as we progress.

Development

I use WordPress as a basis for much of my work. Your design will be implemented as a custom-coded WordPress theme built on my own framework. The coding is search-friendly and concise. I code in:

  • HTML5
  • SASS/Compass and CSS3 (with fallbacks as needed)
  • Javascript and jQuery
  • Custom WordPress coding: Themes and plugins as required
  • Custom PHP / MySQL coding as required

Do you need WordPress plugins for your site? I'll use well-known, vetted plugins that are maintained and don't have security holes.

We'll build the website on a development server, prepare it for launch, and finally launch it when it's ready.

Post-launch

The website is never done. I'll follow up with you to make sure you're successful at implementing the strategy we discussed in the planning stage. In addition, we may find that some aspects of the web strategy or the design of the site aren't engaging users like we thought they would. So there may be some changes to make.
