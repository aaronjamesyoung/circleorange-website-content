About Me

Well, first I was born. I'm from the beautiful Oregon coastal city of Coos Bay. I grew up in a running-crazy family and ran cross country and track for Marshfield High School with some success.

I then attended Willamette University in Salem, continuing to compete in long-distance running while majoring in music. I got married to my beautiful wife Evelyn and lived in Salem for several years. We have a little daughter, Hanna and are planning a move to Bend, Oregon.

Over time I've built my web design skills; first as a hobby, then transitioning to freelance jobs. I've become comfortable with HTML/CSS, PHP & MySQL, and developing for the Wordpress platform. I'm available to build your website for you. See my freelance website at CircleOrange.com.

I am also Design Director for Opt-e-Web, a Salem Oregon web design and technology firm.

I use many open source tools for coding and designing graphics for websites:

  • I design and develop on Arch Linux
  • I use many different text editor / IDE programs, but I'm currently on Geany
  • GIMP and Inkscape for creating graphics
  • Git for version control
  • Wordpress for blogging and content management. I build most client websites with Wordpress.
  • Zim Desktop Wiki for advanced notetaking and todo-lists (this might be my overall favorite app on my computer)

Want to get to know me better? Why don't you Contact Me or follow me on Twitter? I'd love to hear from you.
